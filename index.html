ABOUT

I am currently a Mass Communication Specialist (MC) in the US Navy.

For the past 20 years I've been involved in digital media production for classified and releasable public affairs products.

I've produced content in areas of photojournalism, news writing, videography & editing, still & motion graphic design, social media engagement and desktop publishing.

During my years of service, while stationed around the world and deploying at sea, I became curious to learn about websites, their coding procedures and designs, and how they function. I began taking various courses on front and back-end programming languages, and continue to do so.

In whatever way I can support, I want to build and manage websites, applications and content in creative, technically sound ways. By doing so, I hope to continually learn and progress into a full-stack engineer.

I am a capable, quick learner and believe strongly in my ability to adapt to any work environment. Whether individually or in a team, I believe that success happens when you know when to lead as well as when to follow.
